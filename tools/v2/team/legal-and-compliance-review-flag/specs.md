# Legal and Compliance Review Flag Specs

## Purpose
Self-contained architecture and folder contract for the V2 Team Legal and Compliance Review Flag tool. Enables authorized team members and compliance reviewers to flag mail threads, messages, or assets for legal review, record audit trails, and evaluate compliance categories without touching core application services.
